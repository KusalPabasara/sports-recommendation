## PART A — Participant Information Sheet

### What is this study about?
We are conducting research to develop a computer system that recommends sports to people based on their personal interests, physical strengths, and abilities. This research is part of a machine learning project aimed at helping students and adults discover sports they might enjoy — both for playing and watching.

### Who is running this study?
This study is conducted by [Your Name] from [Department, Institution] as part of an academic research project.

### Who can participate?
You can participate if you are:
- **13 years of age or older**
- Willing to answer questions about your interests, strengths, and sports preferences

> **If you are under 18**: Parental/guardian consent is required in addition to your own agreement. Please ensure a parent or guardian has read and signed the parental consent section before you proceed.

### What will you be asked to do?
You will complete an online survey with approximately **57 questions** across 6 sections:
1. Basic demographics (age, location — no names)
2. Your personal interests and preferences
3. Self-rated physical and cognitive strengths
4. Optional physical measurements (height, weight — approximate)
5. Sports you play, watch, or are curious about
6. Your sporting aspirations

The survey takes approximately **12–15 minutes**.

### What data will be collected?
- **Collected**: Age range, gender, country, interest ratings, strength ratings, physical estimates, sports preferences and engagement.
- **NOT collected**: Your name, email address, phone number, photos, or any identifying information.

All data is collected **anonymously** — we cannot link your responses back to you.

### How will your data be used?
- To train and evaluate a machine learning model for sports recommendation.
- Results will be published in an academic research paper and/or conference.
- Anonymized, aggregated data may be shared publicly on a research repository (e.g., GitHub, Mendeley) for reproducibility.

### Will your data be shared?
Only **anonymized, aggregated** data will be shared. No individual response can be identified. Raw data with any demographic information will be stored securely and deleted after publication.

### Is participation voluntary?
**Yes.** Participation is entirely voluntary. You may:
- Skip any question you are not comfortable answering.
- Stop the survey at any time without consequence.
- Contact the researcher to request removal of your data within **30 days** of submission (after which data cannot be individually identified for removal).

### Are there any risks?
This study involves minimal risk. All questions are about sports preferences and interests — nothing personal, medical, or sensitive beyond basic demographics. If any question makes you uncomfortable, simply skip it.

### Are there any benefits?
You will not receive direct payment for participation. By participating, you contribute to research that may help others — including students — discover sports they enjoy, which could improve physical activity and wellbeing.
